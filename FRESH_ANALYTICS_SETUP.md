## Step 1: Create New Property

1. Go to: https://analytics.google.com
2. Click **Admin** (gear icon, bottom left)
3. In the **Property** column, click **"Create Property"**
4. Fill in:
   - **Property name:** `AI Solutions Hub`
   - **Reporting time zone:** Your timezone
   - **Currency:** USD
5. Click **Next**
6. Fill in business information:
   - Industry category: Technology / Software
   - Business size: Small
   - How you intend to use Google Analytics: Measure customer engagement
7. Click **Create**

## Step 2: Set Up Data Stream

1. After creating property, you'll see **"Set up a data stream"**
2. Click **Web**
3. Fill in:
   - **Website URL:** `https://ai-solutions-hub.vercel.app`
   - **Stream name:** `AI Solutions Hub Website`
4. Click **Create stream**
