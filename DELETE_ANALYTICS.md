## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Google Analytics
1. Visit: https://analytics.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account

### Step 2: Navigate to Admin
1. Click the **Admin** icon (gear icon) in the bottom left corner
2. You'll see three columns: Account, Property, View

### Step 3: Delete the Property
1. In the **Property** column (middle column), make sure "AI Solutions Hub" is selected
2. Click **Property Settings** (top option in Property column)
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
4. Click **Move to Trash Can** (red button at the bottom)
5. Confirm the deletion

**OR Alternative Method:**
1. In the **Property** column, click the dropdown arrow next to "AI Solutions Hub"
2. Look for **"Move to Trash Can"** or **"Delete"** option
3. Click it and confirm

### Step 4: Confirm Deletion
- Google will ask you to confirm
- Type the property name to confirm
- Click **Delete** or **Move to Trash**
