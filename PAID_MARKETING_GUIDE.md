## Paid Marketing Options & Effectiveness

### 1. Google Ads (Search & Display)
**Effectiveness:** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê (Very High)
**Best For:** People actively searching for websites/AI services

**How It Works:**
- Your ads show when people search "website builder", "AI website", "custom website"
- Pay only when someone clicks (Pay-Per-Click)
- Highly targeted - people are already looking for your service

**Cost:**
- $5-$50 per click (varies by keyword)
- Average: $10-$20 per click
- Budget: Start with $500-$1,000/month

**ROI:**
- If 10% of clicks convert to leads = 5-10 leads/month
- If 20% of leads become customers = 1-2 customers/month
- At $949 per site = $949-$1,898 revenue
- **Break-even or profit if you get 1+ customer**

**Effectiveness:** 8/10 - Best for immediate results

### 2. Facebook/Instagram Ads
**Effectiveness:** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê (High)

## How to Get Started

### Option 1: DIY (Save Money)
1. Create Google Ads account
2. Set budget: $500-$1,000/month
3. Target keywords: "website builder", "AI website", "custom website"
4. Create simple text ads
5. Monitor and optimize

**Time:** 5-10 hours setup, 2-3 hours/week management

### Option 2: Hire Freelancer ($500-$1,500/month)
- They set up and manage campaigns
- You pay ad spend + management fee
- Better results, less time

### Option 3: Agency ($2,000-$5,000/month)
- Full service management
- Usually includes ad spend
- Best results, most expensive
